FAQ

Common Questions

We primarily work with pre-seed to seed-stage projects that have a working concept or MVP. Ideally, the founding team has at least 2 members with complementary technical and business skills. We consider projects across all Web3 verticals including DeFi, NFTs, GameFi, Infrastructure, and AI.

Our standard incubation program runs for 6 months, covering everything from technical development and smart contract auditing to community building and exchange listing negotiations. We offer a 3-month accelerator track for more advanced projects closer to launch.

We typically receive an equity stake of 3–8% depending on the stage and scope of support, plus token allocation in projects that have a token component. Our goal is to align incentives for long-term success rather than short-term extraction.
